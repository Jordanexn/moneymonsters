Our Philosophy

We believe in the lifelong benefits financial literacy can provide all people. By turning money management into a playful, rewarding experience, we empower children to develop healthy financial habits that last a lifetime. Money Monsters isn’t just about tracking dollars—it’s about fostering responsibility, discipline, and excitement around saving and earning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between parents and kids when it comes to money. Money Monsters is here to educate children in a fun and engaging way, empower parents to guide their children’s financial growth, and encourage responsible saving by teaching children the value of hard work through rewards.

Features

Set & Track Savings Goals

MoneyMonster's Goal-setting and tracking system, allows children to define savings goals and visually monitor their achievements. This not only teaches them the importance of delayed gratification and planning but also reinforces positive behaviour by showing tangible results of their efforts.

Delegate & Reward Completed Tasks

Parents can assign tasks, set allowances, and tailor financial tasks based on their child’s age and maturity level.These features support habit-building and create a structured yet engaging environment for financial learning.

Seamlessly Monitor Progress

MoneyMonsters integrated family dashboard allows children to seamlessly update their progress for tasks and goals. The parental dashboard reflects the children's updates to ensure that the app aligns with each family’s values and boundaries while also building trust between parent and child
